Getting started

Submit an adoption application to the court

Use this service to complete an application form that has been submitted by the prospective adoptive parents.

You cannot save your progress for the application so it is important you have everything you need before you get started.

What you will need

In order to complete the application and submit it to the court you may need:

  • child's birth certificate
  • any court orders for the child and/or child's siblings
  • Annex A (if relevant)
  • any relevant death certificates
  • last known addresses of the birth parents
  • any other evidence

You will be asked questions about the child's birth parents and siblings. You can review your answers to these questions before you submit the application.

Completing and submitting an application should not take longer than 30 minutes.
