What happens next

The court starts to process your application. The birth parents or anyone with parental responsibility for the child, will be told about the application to adopt but your details will be kept strictly confidential.

There may be several court hearings related to the adoption before the final court hearing. Once the adoption order is granted, an adoption certificate is created. This replaces the child's birth certificate. It can be ordered from the General Register Office for a small fee.

You can sign back in using the email address and password you used when you created your account. You will be emailed notifications of any updates. You can view full details of the updates in your account.
